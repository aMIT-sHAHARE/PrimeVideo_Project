# 📊 Amazon Prime Video Data Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Overview

This project is an end-to-end data analysis of the Amazon Prime Video dataset using Power BI. It focuses on transforming raw data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and visual storytelling.

The dashboard highlights content distribution, genre trends, ratings classification, and growth patterns across different years and countries.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
* Identify the most popular genres
* Study content growth over time
* Understand ratings classification (PG, 13+, 18+)
* Explore country-wise content availability

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Dataset: Amazon Prime Titles
* Format: CSV
* Includes information on title, genre, release year, ratings, and country

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* CSV Dataset

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive dashboard with filters and slicers
* Genre distribution visualization
* Ratings breakdown
* Year-wise trend analysis
* Country-wise insights

## 💡 Key Insights

* Majority of content consists of Movies compared to TV Shows
* Rapid growth in content observed after 2015
* Drama and Comedy are the most dominant genres
* Most content falls under mature audience ratings
* Content production is concentrated in limited regions
